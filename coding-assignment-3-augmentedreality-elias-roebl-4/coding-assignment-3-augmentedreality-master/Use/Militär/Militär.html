Military Use of AR


A lot of money from the US-Government flows into the military
One of the usage of that money is the development of new technologie
So they built very advanced technologies in the area of AR
The new AR technologies shall support the many soldiers in war.
The so called TAR(Tactial Augmented Reality) collects a big amount of data
,and shows it on the AR-helment in real time.


It is also used as a harmless way to train soldiers or
to prepare them for war with simulating these scenarios
and train each soldiers individually to get rid of the weaknesses
and to improve the strengthen.


Microsoft Hololens

The Hololens allows you to visualize troop movements on the battlefield and help soldiers troubleshoot equipment, vehicles, or weapon systems.

Another application is used for the paramedics to simplify their work in an emergency through real-time instructions.

The development of the software is handled on the base of the service model because the developers do not want to waste time to send their developments to the field.

The advantage here is obvious because it avoids the risk that the hardware is not up-to-date and you have to start from the beginning.

Furthermore the Israeli military is in close contact with the Hololens Department and passes on feedback on the hardware to the developers.
