Problems which comes with the use of AR-devices

Augmented reality is a astonishing technologie with nearly unlimited possibilities to use it.
AR could simplify our daily life but with all the benefits come also a lot of dangers.

    Dangers during use of AR

  • It can damage the eye.

    • Because of the permanent use of the devices, our eyes would be overloaded.
    This leads to irreversible damages.(Central vision loss, cataracts,...)

  • It overlays the user

    • AR-devices are throughout collection informations and picture them on your display.
    The user could be distracted by the continious depiction and cause an accident.

  • Every of our movment will be stored

    • The Augmented Reality devices will always store our movment, everything we see and everything we hear. This would destroy our right of privacy.
